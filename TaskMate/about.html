Welcome to TaskMate

TaskMate is a simple and efficient to-do website designed to help you stay organized every day. Whether you're a student, a working professional, or just someone trying to manage personal goals, TaskMate helps you plan, track, and complete your tasks with ease.

The idea behind TaskMate is to make productivity feel light and easy — no clutter, no distractions, just a clean space to focus on what really matters. You can create your daily list, mark completed tasks, and stay consistent in building productive habits.

This website was built by Nitan Sharma as a college web project to explore web development using HTML and CSS. The goal was to create something practical, minimal, and user-friendly.

Future updates will bring more exciting features — reminders, dark mode, and even progress tracking. Stay tuned and make every day productive with TaskMate!
